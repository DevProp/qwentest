About Smart Money Concepts

Smart Money Concepts (SMC) is a trading methodology that focuses on identifying institutional trading patterns and market structure. This tool combines multiple SMC indicators to generate high-probability trading signals with clear entry, stop-loss, and take-profit levels.

How to Use SMC Trader
1. Quick Analysis

Enter any Binance symbol (e.g., BTCUSDT, ETHUSDT) and select your preferred timeframe to get instant SMC analysis.

2. Screener

Use the automated screener to scan all top Binance pairs and find the best trading opportunities based on SMC confluences.

3. Understanding Signals
  • Confidence Score: Higher scores indicate stronger confluence of SMC factors
  • Risk/Reward: Target ratio of at least 1:2 or better
  • Confluences: Multiple SMC factors aligning (Order Blocks, FVGs, Patterns, etc.)
4. Trading Strategy
  • Wait for price to enter discount zone (for buys) or premium zone (for sells)
  • Look for confluence with Order Blocks or Fair Value Gaps
  • Confirm with market structure break (BOS)
  • Set stop loss below/above the Order Block or recent swing
  • Take profit at predefined levels (TP1, TP2, TP3)
